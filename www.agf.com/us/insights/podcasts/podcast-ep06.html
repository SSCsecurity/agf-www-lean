Managing Money in a Crisis

Episode 06 | May 27, 2020

The COVID-19 pandemic has resulted in an economic crisis like no other, but lessons learned from past experiences may help determine the economic landscape that lies ahead. On this episode of Inside Perspectives, Kevin McCreadie, AGF's CEO and Chief Investment Officer, welcomes Greg Valliere, AGF's Chief US Policy Strategist, and John Christofilos, AGF's Chief Trading Officer, to discuss the ins and outs of working from home and managing money in these unprecedented times.
