Anniversary of a Bear Market

Season 2, Episode 3 | March 29, 2021

It’s been one year since markets collapsed from the initial onslaught of the COVID-19 global pandemic, but has the experience of the past year changed us as investors? On this episode of Inside Perspectives, Kevin McCreadie, AGF’s CEO and Chief Investment Officer, is joined by AGF’s Chief Trader John Christofilos as well as Portfolio Manager Steve Bonnyman and Analyst Dillon Culhane to discuss how their particular roles have been affected and what it could mean for investment management going forward.
