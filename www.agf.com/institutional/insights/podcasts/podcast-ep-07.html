Why Tech Hasn’t Been a Wreck During the Recent Crisis

Episode 07 | July 6, 2020

COVID-19 has wreaked havoc on our lives and disrupted our economies, but the fallout could have been worse if not for the technologies that have bridged our ability to work, learn and socialize over the past few months. On this episode of Inside Perspectives, Kevin McCreadie, AGF’s CEO and chief investment officer welcomes AGF equity analyst Auritro Kundu to discuss tech’s role during the pandemic and the innovations that will continue to influence the global economy in its wake.
