Emerging Markets and Trade Wars: Why country diversification matters

On this episode of the podcast, Kevin McCreadie, AGF’s CEO and Chief Investment Officer is joined by Regina Chi, lead portfolio manager of AGF’s emerging market equity strategies to discuss the opportunities and risks of investing in the developing world today.
