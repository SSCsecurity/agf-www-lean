  • 2000s A diversified, international investment firm

  • “Endurance and innovation are the hallmarks of Canada’s best organizations…

    As always, we look to the future but remain rooted by the values and priorities that motivate our clients and investors.”

    – Warren Goldring

  • 2000 Continued expansion

    2000: Global Strategy Financial Inc. acquired
    2005: Global Institutional Account Services team formed

    Also acquired private investment management companies:
    2003: P.J. Doherty & Associates
    2004: Cypress Capital Management
    2006: Highstreet Asset Management Inc.

  • 2002 Sound Choices program for advisors launched
    Originally called AfterWorks, this business-building program – the first for a financial services company in Canada – helps investment advisors and their clients develop a life plan that complements their financial plan.
  • 2005 AGF Elements launched
    A new fund-of-fund product, attracted more than $1 billion in assets its first year, making it one of the most successful products launches in the industry.

  • 2006 and 2008:

    AGF is named Advisors’ Choice Favourite Investment Fund Company of the Year at the Morningstar Canadian Investment Awards

  • 2007 AGF Baby Tiger-Cam at Toronto Zoo

    "AGF was proud to sponsor the AGF Baby Tiger-Cam at the Toronto Zoo and to share the growth and development of these very special babies with Canadians. Through the Baby Tiger-Cam we witnessed the previously hidden world of one of nature's most endangered animals, the Sumatran Tiger."

    Blake Goldring, Executive Chairman, AGF

  • 2007 50 years strong

    The theme of AGF's 50th anniversary – modern thinking, classic performance – captured what the company is all about.

    Innovative thinking has carried AGF through the decades and will continue to carry it well into the future.
