  • 2010s Embracing change
  • 2014 Kevin McCreadie joins AGF

    Kevin McCreadie joined AGF in June 2014 as President and Chief Investment Officer, bringing more than 30 years of experience.
    Kevin provides direction and leadership to AGF’s investment management teams and leads the firm’s global institutional business.

  • 2015 Judy Goldring Top 100 Hall of Fame inductee by WXN

    Judy Goldring, Executive Vice-President and Chief Operating Officer, was named a 2015 Top 100 Hall of Fame Inductee by the Women's Executive Network™ (WXN).

    This honour is given to women who are nominated as one of Canada’s Most Powerful Women: Top 100 for a fourth time.

  • 2015 Ownership in ETF Strategist

    In November 2015, AGF announced that it had taken an ownership stake in ETF strategist FFCM, a Boston-based ETF advisor and asset management firm whose expertise is delivered through a family of alternative and smart-beta ETFs and a number of ETF managed solutions.

  • 2016 AGF CustomerFirst

    February 2016: AGF made a significant investment in client service with the launch of “AGF CustomerFirst” which provides call centre and customer support.

    As the name implies, we are putting our clients first and have taken the customer experience to a new level.

  • 2017 Introducing ETFs

    Leveraging our quantitative investment capabilities, AGF – under the AGFiQ brand, a new subsidiary – launched seven actively managed ETFs.

    Enhanced core: Cdn Equity / U.S. Equity / Int'l Equity / EM Equity

    Multi-asset, outcome-oriented: Global Equity Rotation / Multi Asset Allocation / Multi Asset Income

  • 2017 #MakingADifference

    At AGF, our mission of helping investors succeed has always extended to our communities.

    In 2017, for our 60th anniversary, we're recognizing the efforts of our employees to make a difference in their communities.

    Visit AGF.com/Facebook on Mondays to find out how AGF is #MakingADifference.
